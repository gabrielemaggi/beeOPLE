beeOPLE

beeOPLE is a web application designed for a simple and fast comunication above the network with people from all the world!!!.It's the first social network inspired from natural enviroment; in particular th e world of bees. We think that bees phenomenon is very interesting because it shows us the particular system of comunications used in bees's hive to trasmit different signals.To do this, bese use a lot of different dance to comunicate. So, like the bees dance to comunicate, we want you to comunicate to other people with beeOPLE for tell at your "hive" some problems or places you which other people will see!
