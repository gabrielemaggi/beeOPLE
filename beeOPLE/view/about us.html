Who are we?

Hi everyone, we are the creators of beeOPLE; we are fifth and fourth year students of ITIS P.Paleocapa located in Bergamo who wanted to try their hand at this particular challenge. With this website we want to strengthen communication between municipalities, which is disappearing more and more with time, in fact thanks to it you will be able to "dance" like bees in your "hive" to let the other "bees" know what is happening in your municipality.
